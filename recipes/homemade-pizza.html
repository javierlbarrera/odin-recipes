Homemade pizza

An Italian classic you can bake in your own oven.

You can either do it with homemade dough or not, but it will taste so much better with that dough.



We will teach you how to make your own dough - the steps afterwards are fairly easy and will depend on the ingredients you want to add to your pizza. We will be making the classical margherita pizza

Ingredients

for your homemade dough

  • Flour (500g)
  • Water (350ml)
  • A package of active dry yeast
  • Salt (a pinch)
  • Olive oil (a teaspoon)

for the toppings

  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil

Alright, let's start with the recipe:

  1. Mix the ingredients for the dough and knead it for a while until everything is fully incorporated.
  2. Let the dough rise until it's doubled its size.
  3. Once it's nice and fluffy, spread it and add your toppings of choice to it.
  4. Bake in the oven at max heat until it's fully cooked
  5. Enjoy!
